Automated 3-2-1 Backup.

Our two-part system creates three instant backups: one on your camera, one on portable storage, and one in the cloud. Your editor can start working moments after you take the shot.

01 / CAPTURE

Capture Module

A small, rugged device that mounts to your camera's hot shoe and connects via USB-C. It instantly pulls a copy of every shot, eliminating the memory card as a single point of failure.

  • → Hot shoe mounting system
  • → Real-time USB-C image capture
  • → Weather-resistant design

02 / RELAY

Network Relay Station

The portable hub that receives images from the Capture Module and immediately creates three backups: one on a hot-swappable SSD, a second on a redundant HDD, and a third to the cloud.

  • → Hot-swappable SSD storage
  • → Redundant internal HDD
  • → Wi-Fi, 5G & Ethernet sync
